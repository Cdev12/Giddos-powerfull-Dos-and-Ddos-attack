Overview
GiDDOS-HackSoftware 3.0 is a state-of-the-art cybersecurity tool designed to simulate advanced DDoS (Distributed Denial of Service) and DoS (Denial of Service) attacks, alongside a variety of stress testing functions for evaluating the resilience of web infrastructure. Developed for penetration testers, ethical hackers, and cybersecurity professionals, this software offers powerful features for testing and fortifying your systems against modern cyber threats.

DISCLAIMER: This software is intended for use on systems that you own or have been explicitly authorized to test. Misuse of this tool is prohibited by law.

Key Features
Booter (DoS & DDoS)
A robust tool designed to simulate DoS and DDoS attacks to test the stability and response of your infrastructure under heavy traffic conditions.

Denial of Service (DoS)
Simulate DoS attacks by flooding systems with traffic to test their ability to withstand service disruptions.

Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS)
Leverage multiple attack sources to simulate large-scale DDoS attacks, testing your defenses against distributed network threats.

Stresser
A sophisticated stress-testing tool used to push your network's capabilities to the limit. Identify potential vulnerabilities and assess performance under load.

Cybersecurity Testing
Simulate real-world cyberattacks to discover weaknesses in your infrastructure and implement necessary security measures before they are exploited.

TestYourOwnSite
A specialized feature to evaluate the security and robustness of your own website or server by performing controlled, safe stress tests.
